Transactional Emails are emails that are typically related to system and account related activity. A common example of these types of emails are Password emails, system notifications, and email verification messages.

The parts of a Transactional Email are:

  • Headers - The information that tells email servers how to route emails, who they are for, and what to do with them.
  • Content - The actual information that the customer sees within the email.
  • Template - The "pretty" part of the email, everything that is not content that the user sees.
